Hello!

Welcome to RazeBot, a tournament bot for Discord.


RazeBot is a tournament and matchmaking bot for Discord gaming servers.
It creates brackets for games, creates servers for the games, saves stats for each game, keeps track of player levels, verifies game results and has voice output to the contestants.
Basically, its an all-in-one tournament organiser... and its awesome.
